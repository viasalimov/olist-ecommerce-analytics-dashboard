Dataset: Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist

Source:
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/olistbr/brazilian-ecommerce

Description:
The dataset contains Brazilian e-commerce marketplace data from Olist, including orders, products, customers, sellers, payments, reviews, and delivery information.

Period:
2016–2018

Dataset Size:
~100,000 orders

Used for:
Power BI dashboard development, data modeling, business analytics, and visualization.
